What is Kooch?

Kooch application was the first project we worked on in UX class. Three of us chose this project and designed it.

The application is all about travel and allows you to plan according to the number of days you will be traveling. The app shows you all the tourist attractions of the destination city, as well as the tourist routes that you can take so you can make the most of the trip every day without wasting time looking for the routes

User research

An online survey was distributed to people to obtain basic information.

We received 50 responses. Fifty-five percent of respondents travel more than three times a year. Seventy-three percent travel for leisure. Before traveling, most people checked the weather, accommodations, and access routes.

In an interview with ten respondents who filled out the survey, we learned the following:

  • Travel preferences and reasons
  • Travel challenges
  • Travel costs
  • Travel limitations
  • Travel app expectations
